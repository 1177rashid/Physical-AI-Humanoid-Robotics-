# AI-Native Textbook on Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the comprehensive textbook on Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics! This interactive textbook covers the essential concepts needed to understand and implement embodied AI systems that connect digital intelligence to humanoid robots.

## About This Textbook

This textbook is designed for advanced students and AI engineers who want to learn about:
- Physical AI systems that connect digital intelligence to humanoid robots
- Using ROS 2, Gazebo, NVIDIA Isaac, and Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models
- Designing, simulating, and deploying autonomous humanoid robots

## What You'll Learn

Through this textbook, you will:
- Understand sensing, perception, planning, and control for humanoid robots
- Work with ROS 2 nervous system architectures
- Explore digital twins and simulation environments
- Master NVIDIA Isaac platforms
- Integrate Vision-Language-Action models
- Control humanoid robots effectively
- Complete a voice-driven humanoid capstone project

## Navigation

The textbook is organized in a modular structure aligned with ROS 2, Digital Twins, Isaac, and VLA concepts. You can navigate through the sections using the sidebar to the left.

## Hands-On Experience

This textbook includes hands-on lab exercises and culminates in a voice-driven humanoid capstone project that integrates all concepts learned throughout the course.
